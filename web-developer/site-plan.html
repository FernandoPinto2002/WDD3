Packages

In order to be able to select the best option for you, we have created our packages the basic, standard, and premium. each of these packages comes along with features and bonuses. We prioritize the packages in order of cost, this does mean we are not going to leave basic packages behind but the importance is based on how much the client is willing to contribute to his webpage. We have the Basic package, which consists of a professionally designed webpage with a customizable template, up to 5 pages (Home, About Us, Services, Contact, Blog), mobile-responsive design for optimal viewing on all devices, secure hosting for one year long wait for customer support via email. The standard Package includes everything in the basic package plus up to 10 pages, including additional features like testimonials or portfolio, priority customer support via email and phone, and advanced SEO optimization for improved search engine visibility. The premium package has everything in the standard package plus a fully bespoke webpage design, crafted to your specifications, unlimited pages and features, including advanced functionalities like interactive elements or custom forms, dedicated account manager for personalized support and guidance, and priority access to technical support with fast response times.
